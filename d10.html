Event Description

Are you tired of your classes that you have been attending? Do you want to have something fun and thrilling that can elevate your thinking? Come and challenge yourself where your wits will be put to test. The first team to crack all the clues will be the winner.

Rules

1. A team should consist of only 4 members. Open to all UG and PG students.
2. Late Registrations won't be entertained.
3. All the members of the team should be from the same institute.
4. Participants should always carry their college ID.
5. Team should report at the venue 30 minutes before commencement of the event.
6. Spot registration closes at 10 AM on 16th October 2022.
7. Team members participating in the treasure hunt will not be allowed to participate in other events.
