AGRATA'22

Agrata is a State level management fest conducted by Department of Business Administration in Providence Women’s College, Calicut. A management fest that conducts management and non-management activities. It is an event to testify the management qualities of students and to embark a competitive spirit.

2 - 3 DEC 2022

ABOUT COLLEGE



Providence Women’s College, Calicut,
is one of the largest higher education centers for women in the state of Kerala. It was established in 1952 by the sisters of Apostolic Carmel, to create empowerment through education among the women folk in the Malabar region. It was affiliated to the Madras university in 1952 and later on it became part of Kerala university in 1958 and finally it was brought under the university of Calicut in 1968. The college provides post graduate courses, under graduation courses. Along with the A+ grade awarded by NAAC; the college has also been granted the status of CPE – ‘College with potential for Excellence’ by the UGC.
